Wooden mirrors

Handcrafted with love, made with perfection

We are „RustikO” – the designer and producer of charming furniture made from solid wood. All our furniture is completely handcrafted with a big passion for woodworking. That’s why each piece of our furniture has its own distinctive style and mood.

Many years of our experience in the woodworking field ensure the high quality and well-thought-out design of all our wooden mirrors and rustic furniture.

Become inspired by the mystery spirit of wood...

100% HANDMADE

Our solid wood furniture is fully handmade with a brushed surface in a rustic style.

ALWAYS UNIQUE

Each piece of wood we carefully select has its own uniqueness because of individual wood texture, distinctive knots, tiny cracks, and other special wood features, so our furniture is unique and one of a kind.

NATURAL PRODUCTS

During the production of our wooden furniture, we use only natural materials such as wood, metal, and wax oils. We use wood that is sourced sustainably.

TIMELESS STYLE

The natural warmth and beauty of wood add a touch of elegance and sophistication to any space and style, from classic and traditional to modern and contemporary.

MADE TO LAST

Handcrafted thoroughly with attention to every detail, each piece of Rustiko furniture is made with high-quality materials to last for generations.
