Wall oakwood mirrors

Explore our wall mirrors made from solid wood and designed to be easily hung on the wall vertically or horizontally to create an authentic wood mood in every home.

Our mirrors are fully handcrafted from oakwood with a hand-brushed surface and are available in a natural appearance of wood, like knots & cracks in a rustic style, or in a smooth finish for an elegant and minimalist look.

All our mirrors can be hung vertically or horizontally due to three special hangers applied on the backside of the mirrors. Or they can simply stand and lean against the wall without hanging in order to fit different needs.

Our collection of mirrors comes in different sizes and finishes:

  • standard sizes are 150x65/60 cm, 120x65/60 cm, 100x80 cm 90x65 cm, and 80x60 cm of height and width,
  • also, we can make any other size according to Your needs, up to 200 cm in length and width.

Frames are available in different widths: 70 and 90 mm.

The mirror frames are coated and protected with top-quality moisture-resistant wax oils to provide a long-lasting, durable finish. Colors: Light Oak, Honey Oak, Antique Oak, and others.

We use 4mm-deep silvered mirror glass from leading European producers.

Safety backing: a special safety film is applied on the back to prevent glass scattering in the event of accidental breakage.

The backside of our mirrors is excellently finished with HDF board and aluminum U-profile.

Oak coffee tables

Explore our beautiful coffee tables with round tops made from solid oakwood and fully handcrafted to create an authentic wood mood in every home.

Our tables are fully handmade with a hand-brushed or smooth surface and are available in a natural appearance of wood, like knots & cracks in a rustic style, or in a smooth finish for an elegant and minimalist look.

We use solid oak wood or ash wood to make our tables.

Tables are available with a beautiful brushed surface or can be smooth finished.

They are coated and protected with top-quality moisture-resistant wax oils to provide a long-lasting, durable finish. Colors: Light Oak, Honey Oak or Antique Oak.

Our coffee tables come in different sizes:

  • diameters: 53 cm, 60 cm and 70 cm,
  • and the regular height is 43-45 cm,
  • the thickness of table tops is 24 mm.
  • also, we can make any other size according to Your needs.

Table tops are available with square or chamferred edges.

Tables come with four straight or angled tapered legs.

The legs are easy to install: every leg is equipped with mounting metal plates and 4 screws, so the legs can be easily assembled to the table.

Floor oakwood mirrors

Explore our floor-standing mirrors made from solid wood and equipped on the back with a forged metal bracket finished with a patina effect.

The frames of our mirrors are fully handcrafted from oakwood with a hand-brushed surface and are available in a natural appearance of wood, like knots & cracks, in a rustic style.

All our metal brackets are individually handmade, forged and coated with a patina effect in black & bronze colors.

The metal bracket on the back is removable and easy to install. Due to this, our mirrors have wide applications and can be used as free-standing floor mirrors or mounted on the wall.

The bracket is designed in a special way to make inclination angle adjustments of the mirror reflection possible and very simple.

Our floor mirrors come in a standard size of 160x55 cm in height and width, but other customized sizes are possible as well.

Mirror frames are available in two different widths: 70 and 90 mm.

The mirror frames are coated and protected with top-quality moisture-resistant wax oils to provide a long-lasting, durable finish. Colors: Light Oak, Honey Oak, Antique Oak, and others.

We use 4 mm-deep silvered mirror glass from leading European producers.

Safety backing: a special safety film is applied on the back to prevent glass scattering in the event of accidental breakage.

The backside of our mirrors is excellently finished with HDF board and aluminum U-profile.

This kind of full-length mirrors are ideal for studios, fitting rooms, bedrooms, halls, or any other rooms.
